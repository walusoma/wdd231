Buwenge Youth Football Academy

Site Plan Document

1. Site Name

Buwenge Youth Football Academy

This name represents a youth football training academy in Buwenge focused on developing local talent.

2. Site Purpose

The website provides football training information, team updates, player registration, and academy news for young players in Buwenge.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I join the football academy?
  • What training sessions are available each week?

4. Color Scheme

#0b3d91
Primary
#f4b400
Accent

Blue is used for headers and navigation. Yellow is used for highlights and buttons.

5. Typography

Font: Arial (used for all text)
Headings: Bold Arial
Body: Regular Arial for readability

6. Wireframe

Mobile: Header → Menu → Content → Footer

Desktop: Header → Side navigation → Main content → Footer
